## Game Tips

### Brewing Strategy
1. **Use the Notebook:** After brewing a few teas, check the Tea Notes section for your personal best brewing parameters
2. **Watch the Timer:** The progress bar changes color to indicate quality (green = perfect, yellow = good, red = poor)
3. **Match Customer Preferences:** Pay attention to what tea types each customer personality prefers
4. **Patience Matters:** Artists and Merchants have low patience (2 min), while Elders and Enthusiasts will wait longer (5 min)

### Temperature Guidelines (Quick Reference)
- **60-65°C:** Gyokuro (premium green)
- **70-80°C:** Most green teas, white teas
- **85-95°C:** Oolong teas, some premium whites
- **95-100°C:** Black teas, Pu-erh

### Foraging Locations
- **Valley:** White teas, Jasmine Pearls
- **Forest:** Oriental Beauty, Golden Yunnan
- **Mountain:** Dragon Well, Tieguanyin, Da Hong Pao, Lapsang Souchong
- **Hidden Grove:** Gyokuro, Aged Pu-erh (legendary teas)

### Earning Strategy
1. Start with common teas to build reputation
2. Perfect brews earn 50% more coins
3. Save money to unlock foraging locations
4. Rare teas have higher base prices but require more precision
5. Match customers with their preferred tea types for better satisfaction

### Using the Notebook
- **Tea Notes:** Shows your success rate and best parameters for each tea
- **Customer Log:** Track which customers liked which teas
- **Brew History:** Review your recent performance
- **General Notes:** Record your own observations
- **Statistics:** Monitor your overall progress and most-brewed teas
